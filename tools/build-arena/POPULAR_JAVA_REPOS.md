## Priority Testing Recommendations

### Small Projects (Fast builds, <1 minute)
Perfect for quick demos and testing:
- spring-projects/spring-petclinic
- apache/commons-lang
- apache/commons-io
- google/gson
- square/okio
- joda-time/joda-time

### Medium Projects (1-5 minutes)
Good performance comparison candidates:
- google/guava
- square/okhttp
- square/retrofit
- junit-team/junit5
- mockito/mockito
- google/guice

### Large Projects (5-15 minutes)
Show Elide's impact on complex builds:
- spring-projects/spring-boot
- spring-projects/spring-framework
- apache/kafka
- elastic/elasticsearch
- gradle/gradle

### Very Large Projects (15+ minutes)
Dramatic speedup demonstrations:
- apache/spark
- apache/hadoop
- apache/flink

## Notes

- Most Spring projects use Gradle
- Most Apache projects use Maven
- Google projects typically use Maven
- Square projects typically use Gradle
- All these projects have CI/CD and are actively maintained
- Build times vary based on test execution, compilation, and packaging
